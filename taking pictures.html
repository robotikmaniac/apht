Get out there and shoot some pictures. Even if its with just a simple
point and shoot camera. The beauty of photography in the digital age is that
you no longer need to buy a roll of film with a very limited amount of picture
space. Now, with a digital camera, or even a smart phone, you have virtually an
unlimited number of pictures at your disposal. If you take 100 pictures, but, only
like 5 of your shots, you can easily erase the other 95 pictures and move on.
