Big Sky Photo Adventures

By creating this website, I hope to share pictures, stories, and adventures
from here in Montana,
my home, The Big Sky Country. Here you will able to see cool pictures,
as well as read stories of
adventures that have been had.

You will also be able to share your own stories. Have a question
about whats happening in Montana? Ask it on the interactive page
and get an answer. Photography is my passion, and thats will be
my primary area of focus on this site.
I will be adding picture ideas as well
shooting tips and what type of equipment I like to use.
Capturing a special moment in time is a forever keepsake that
you will look upon with fondness and memory inducing feelings
for the rest of your life. I am proud to share some of my pictures
and stores with all of you,
