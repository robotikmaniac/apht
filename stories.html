Mother Nature loves to show off in Montana

Storm

This is a picture I took of a summer storm approaching from the west. Just past the storm lie the Rocky Mountains.
Thunderstorms in Montana can be quite fearsome as the mountains act as
giant wind generators.

Love is Colorblind

These two swans make
their home on the pond at Gibson Park in Great Falls. The park was named after the cities
founder, Paris Gibson. The swans pictured live a life of luxury that affords them warm housing
in the winter, and, a nice comfy pond for swimming in the summer. The swans are always a big
crowd pleaser at the park.
